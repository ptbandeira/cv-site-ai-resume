How Scout Works (simplified)
  • Ingest: RSS feeds → SharePoint → Power Automate (6-hourly scan)

  • Deliver: Teams Adaptive Cards with confidence badges + action buttons
  • Track: Partner clicks "Act Now" → pre-drafted email + CRM logging

  • SharePoint = knowledge base, Teams = delivery, Power Automate = orchestration
  • Enterprise security & compliance built-in (critical for law firms)
  • Path to Microsoft Marketplace = scalable distribution
