Remember when everyone worried about the internet replacing fax machines overnight? AI isn't a digital meteor strike, it's a new switchboard operator for your business.

This market drama about "ghost GDP" and AI agents by 2028 is mostly noise for your European SMB. While AI will certainly reshape workflows, it's not a sudden collapse of your business model.

Your competitive edge isn't built on reacting to Substack fiction. It's built on strategically integrating AI to automate repetitive tasks and improve service. Think of it as upgrading your filing system, not throwing out all your files.

Ignore the theatrics. Identify 2-3 specific, repetitive tasks. Pilot AI tools there. Measure tangible savings over 3-6 months.

Takeaway for executives: Focus on controlled, incremental AI adoption for specific operational gains, not market panic.
