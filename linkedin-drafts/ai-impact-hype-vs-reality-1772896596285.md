# LinkedIn Draft — For you, a European SMB owner, this cuts through a lot of the noise. It confirms what I've been saying: the immediate, widespread job displacement predicted by some AI evangelists isn't happening. The gap between what AI *can* do and what people *are actually using it for* is substantial. Your most experienced, highly-paid staff aren't being replaced tomorrow; their roles are evolving.

Your most experienced staff aren't being replaced by AI next week.

Forget the headlines. The gap between what AI *can* do and what people *are actually using it for* is still a Grand Canyon. New data from Anthropic confirms this: AI is not yet the digital switchboard operator handling all your calls.

Your high-paid experts are not becoming obsolete. Their roles are evolving, shedding the repetitive, manual data entry tasks that feel like sifting through endless file cabinets for a single document.

Stop chasing theoretical AI capabilities. Pinpoint 2-3 high-volume tasks, especially those done by senior team members, and pilot a specific AI tool. Prove real ROI in weeks, not years, like a focused 4-week sprint to digitize your Rolodex.

The immediate win is augmentation, not replacement. Focus your AI pilots on high-value task automation for existing staff.
