Your data isn't always in the cloud. Google's latest Android update puts powerful AI directly on your phone. Think of it as upgrading your secure file cabinet to an AI-powered one, but it stays locked in your office.

For law firms, consultancies, or accounting practices, this is critical. Summarizing calls or drafting sensitive notes with AI can now happen locally. No more sending client data out to a big server farm to get work done.

It means secure, AI-powered productivity without the usual data sovereignty headaches. You get the efficiency without compromising client trust, which is a non-negotiable in our line of work.
