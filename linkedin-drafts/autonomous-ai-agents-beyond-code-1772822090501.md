What Symphony truly signifies is the rapid advancement of robust, autonomous AI agents capable of executing complex, multi-step workflows with built-in verification. The underlying engineering principles here—self-correction, structured execution, proof-of-work—are what will eventually empower powerful, industry-specific AI assistants for services like yours.

These aren't just macros. They're digital switchboard operators, capable of routing entire processes, self-correcting errors, and proving their work, from client onboarding to contract review. They're not just filing documents; they're understanding and acting on them.

Ignore the technical jargon. Your focus needs to be on meticulously documenting every repetitive, multi-stage process in your firm. Just like preparing a new filing system before the documents arrive.

Map your multi-step operational workflows today. This will position you to leverage sophisticated agentic capabilities when they inevitably arrive for professional services, likely within 12 to 24 months.
